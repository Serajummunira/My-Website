Q6: Process Mapping - Kabaddi Website Concept

a. Distinctive Features and Functionality

The website will be a one-stop hub for kabaddi fans, offering real-time match updates, player statistics, live streaming options, and a forum for community discussions. It will also provide an interactive tutorial section for new players, along with a dedicated marketplace for kabaddi-related gear and merchandise.

Key Features:

  • Live Scores & Match Updates: Real-time updates on kabaddi tournaments and league matches.
  • Player & Team Stats: Detailed statistics for players and teams in different leagues.
  • Live Streaming: Integrated links for streaming kabaddi matches.
  • Community Forum: A space for discussions, predictions, and fan engagement.
  • Training & Tutorials: Video tutorials and strategy guides for new and experienced players.
  • Merchandise Store: A marketplace for jerseys, shoes, and kabaddi accessories.
