Spaghetti Diagram - Getting My Degree

Project Description

This project represents the steps involved in earning a Master's Degree in Information Quality at UALR. The spaghetti diagram will map the studentâ€™s journey through various university systems, interactions with faculty and advisors, and necessary administrative steps.

Steps in the Degree Process

  • Apply for Admission: Submit an application online, send transcripts, and wait for admission decision.
  • Enroll in Courses: Register for courses through the student portal.
  • Attend Classes: Join lectures, complete assignments, and participate in discussions.
  • Complete Assignments: Submit coursework and research projects.
  • Maintain GPA: Ensure good academic standing for program completion.
  • Complete Capstone: Work on a final research project or thesis.
  • Apply for Graduation: Submit necessary paperwork and clearance forms.
  • Graduate: Receive final degree confirmation and attend the graduation ceremony.
